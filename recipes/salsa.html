Salsa

Description:

Once upon a time, I lived in Cedar City and found the greatest salsa on earth at a restaurant named La Fiesta. This is my attempt to replicate it. You need onions and cilantro if you want it to be close. It's magical.

Ingredients:

  • I use canned tomatoes, unless(!) it's tomato season and I can grab some from the garden.
  • 1 28 oz. can Whole Tomatoes
  • 2-3 Garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 Whole Onion, diced small (Yellow, white, or red. You choose!)
  • 2 Limes
  • Bunch of cilantro. Like... A bunch.
  • Chilies of choice -- I use anaheims for body, serranos for heat
  • Salt
  • Cumin
  • Pepper

Directions:

  1. Put all ingredients except cilantro in blender. Pulse to desired consistency.
  2. Chop cilantro and stir in.
  3. Just kidding. That's all. That's it.
    • Eat within two days. You can use the leftovers to make spanish rice or salsa chicken.
