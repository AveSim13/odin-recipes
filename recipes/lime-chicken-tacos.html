Lime Chicken Tacos

Description:

Simple and scrumptious, this taco recipe will be one of your go-tos for all eternity. Quick to make, nice caramelized flavor packed in to some perfectly browned chicken pieces.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 - Chopped Boneless Chicken Breasts
  • 1/8 cup (child's splash) - Red Wine Vinegar
  • 1-2 - Limes (split for juice)
  • 1 tsp. - White sugar
  • To taste - Salt
  • To taste - Pepper
  • 1/2 - Onion (diced small)
  • 2 Cloves - Garlic
  • 1 tsp. - Oregano
  • Tortillas
  • Toppings

Directions:

  1. Cover bottom of medium pan in oil. Sweat onions and garlic on medium heat for 2-3 minutes.
  2. Raise heat, add diced chicken. You want to brown the meat.
    • More Brown = More Flavor
  3. Once browned, add red wine vinegar, juice of lime(s) (to taste... I like limes so I do a lot), salt, pepper, oregano, and sugar.
  4. Continue cooking. I like to go until the mixture starts to caramelize.
  5. Serve in warm tortillas w/ sour cream, tomoatoes, lettuce, and cheese.
