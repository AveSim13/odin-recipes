Breakfast Tacos

Description:

These tacos were a college staple. Simple. Healthy. Filling. And quick to make. One of these will leave you wanting more.

Ingredients:

  • 2-4 Eggs
  • 1 Serrano Pepper (can sub jalapeno if you can't handle heat)
  • 1/4 Onion diced small
  • 1-2 Tomatoes diced
  • 1/2 Bell Pepper diced
  • 1 Garlic clove minced
  • Salt
  • Corn Tortillas
  • Salsa/hot sauce of choice
  • Cheese
  • Sour Cream

Directions:

  1. Chop those aromatics (onion, garlic, chiles) and throw them into the fooping pan. Drizzle them with some olive oil to keep them company.
  2. I like to keep these to one pan, so push the veg to the top and start scrambling some eggs in the empty space. Once scrambled, combine with veg.
  3. Add tomatoes (not a veg) and cook for about 2 minutes. Pan should be on low heat now. Season with salt.
  4. Crisp up some corn tortillas with a bit of oil in a small pan, and fill each with egg mixture. Top with sour ream and salsa and slam those babies into your eater.
